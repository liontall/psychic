Telepathy.

Connect with minds and convey ideas without physcial communication.

Telepathy is defined in the dictionary as follows: 'the supposed communication of thoughts or ideas by other means than the known senses.' Many people have had moments in their lives where the phone rang at the exact moment when you think of someone. It's often said of twins that they can feel when the other is in danger, even if they are miles apart. For some people, it even goes a step further and they can actually tune in to non-sensory vibrations and energies in the cosmis layers. This allows to convey messages and interact with another's mind or spirit without physical communication. Experiments are hard to reproduce and hence this is considered a pseudoscience.

Remote Viewing.

Connect with minds and convey ideas without physcial communication.

A practice developed by the CIA during the cold war whereby a psychic person gathers intelligence about a specific person, place or object from a remote distance. This ability assumes connection to the cosmic energy layers which can be accessed through meditation. People with this ability are able to see images and contextual information about a specific situation or concrete question. Remote viewing is closely related to the concept of clarivoyance and mediumship. Many remote viewers believe that anyone can develop their inner psychic, and training programs are available to those who are interested to learn more.

Medium.

'A person claiming to be in contact with the spirits of the dead and to communicate between the dead and the living.'

Allen Kardec was a French professor and the founder of the occult movement of 'Spiritism'. He wrote 5 books known as 'the codification of the spirit world'. One one his books, 'Le livre des Esprits' (1857), was written in cooperation with 12 people with the gift of mediumship. In the book, a series of questions are asked to specific spirits - Napopleon Bonaparte and Saint Augustine among them. Spiritism is the third largest religion in Brazil. Today many educational programs exist for mediums. The Arthur Finley college in the UK is the most famous school.
